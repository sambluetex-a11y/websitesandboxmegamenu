BlueTex Insulation

BlueTex helps metal building owners solve heat, condensation, and damaged insulation problems with practical foil/white foam insulation systems.

Use Pro 2mm and Supreme 6mm roll products, reinforced seam tapes, EasyClip hangers, and aluminum TermBars to create a clean, durable system for shops, warehouses, barns, garages, roll-up doors, and cover/repair projects.
